— Mobile Phone Keypad Cipher —

Detective's note: Found scribbled on a beer-soaked receipt. The numbers spell letters the way an old mobile-phone keypad does — press a number once, twice, or three times to pick a letter (eg. 2 = A, 22 = B, 222 = C). The ">" means the next letter is on the same key, so move on. Type the decoded word in each blue box.
