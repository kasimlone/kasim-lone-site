CLUE #5

— Mixed Cipher: A Real Puzzle —

Detective's note: This one's a doozy. The perp mixed all the previous cipher styles into a single message:

  • Letters — Caesar cipher (work out the shift yourself!)
  • Single digits / two-digit numbers — could be number cipher (1=A, 2=B, …) or a single phone-keypad press. Try both.
  • Long digit strings (e.g. 444, 22277…) — phone keypad multi-tap. ">" means next press is on the same key.
  • Dots & dashes — Morse code.

Type each decoded chunk into the blue box below it. The cell may hold one letter or several.
